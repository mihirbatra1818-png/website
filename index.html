Quick Review
How to Play

Type the first aid passages as fast and accurately as possible. After each round, the slowest typists are eliminated.

Last responder standing wins. Accuracy counts — errors slow you down.

The 3 Steps
CHECK
Ensure the scene is safe. Check for responsiveness. Assess the situation.
CALL
Call 9-1-1 or have someone call. Activate the emergency response system.
CARE
Provide first aid until help arrives. CPR, bleeding control, rescue breathing.
